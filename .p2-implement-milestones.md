## Architecture notes discovered (trust-but-verified on disk)

- `recall()` (pluresdb.ts ~926) returns ONLY direct vector+text hits. **Graph
  expansion is NOT in recall()** — it happens downstream in
  `memory-capability.ts::createPluresLmSearchManager().search()` (~line 327+),
  which takes `hits.slice(0,3)` as seeds and APPENDS graph neighbors AFTER all
  direct hits, de-duped, never displacing a direct hit. → My salience change to
  recall()'s sort only reorders DIRECT hits among themselves; the P1
  precision guardrail (graph neighbor never displaces direct top-1) is
  structurally untouched.
- `#writeCheckpoint` persists `{lastRunEpoch,runs,edges,clusters,topRanked}`.
- `#readCheckpoint` (~1150) read back ONLY `{lastRunEpoch,runs}` → topRanked
  orphaned. This is the reader bug P2-0 fixes.
